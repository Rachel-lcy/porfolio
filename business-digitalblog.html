✨ Rachel's Digital Blog – Business Insights

Project Overview

Rachel's Digital Blog is a personal tech blog and portfolio platform that showcases web development tutorials, creative coding projects, and digital insights. It is designed to highlight Rachel's technical journey and provide value to aspiring developers through authentic, well-structured content.

Target Audience

  • Beginner to intermediate developers looking for tutorials and inspiration
  • Tech enthusiasts interested in full-stack development and digital tools
  • Recruiters and potential collaborators evaluating technical skills

Tech Stack

Node.js | Express.js | MongoDB | EJS

Business Problem

Individual developers and freelancers often lack:

  • A centralized, well-designed portfolio for sharing projects and insights
  • A platform to grow a professional identity and attract job/internship opportunities
  • Custom control over SEO, blog layout, and personal branding

How the Website Solves These Problems

Project Showcase

- A clean layout for publishing code-based projects with descriptions and visuals.

Custom Blog Engine

- Built-in blog functionality using EJS and MongoDB to allow flexible article publishing.

SEO + Branding

- Meta tags, routing, and personal domain improve discoverability and professionalism.

Backend Control

- Node.js + Express.js structure allows Rachel to manage routing, content, and logic.

Key Features

  • In-home pet sitting reservation system
  • Online product catalog and checkout flow
  • Custom CMS for managing listings and content
  • Clean, responsive UI with modern design principles
  • Business insight section that simulates real-world decision-making

Result

Through this platform, Rachel built a personal brand as a web developer, improved online presence, and attracted more networking and job interview opportunities by actively sharing high-quality technical content.
