Project Overview

Floatin is a playful, social networking application designed to make meeting new people effortless and enjoyable. Inspired by the lightness and joy of floating bubbles, the platform offers a unique way to connect through icebreaker games, personality-based matching, and community events. The goal is to reduce the awkwardness of first conversations and help users form meaningful connections naturally.

Target Audience

  • Young adults and professionals looking to expand their social circles
  • People new to a city who want to meet locals and join activities
  • Users who enjoy playful, gamified interaction rather than traditional dating apps
  • Community builders seeking fun ways to organize and promote events

Tech Stack

HTML, CSS, JS,Clerk, builder.io, Vercel, Canvas

Business Problem

Many people face challenges in forming new connections:

  • Awkwardness and pressure during first-time conversations
  • Limited opportunities to meet like-minded individuals
  • Overreliance on traditional dating apps that lack a casual, community-driven approach

How the Website Solves These Problems

Icebreaker Games

- Engages users with fun and unique conversation starters, making the first interaction light and stress-free.

Personality-Based Matching

- Uses quizzes and compatibility scores to help users find like-minded friends.

Community Meetup Events

- Encourages in-person or group interactions by providing a platform for organizing and joining themed events.

Mobile-Responsive UI

- Ensures users can connect anytime, anywhere, with an intuitive, playful interface.

Trust-Building Design

- Soft gradients, bubble imagery, and friendly illustrations create a welcoming atmosphere.

Key Features

  • Bubble-tap matching system for discovering new people
  • Gamified icebreaker activities
  • Personality quiz and compatibility-based suggestions
  • Event creation and participation tools
  • Clean, mobile-friendly design with engaging visuals

Result

Floatin helps users break free from small talk stress, leading to increased event participation, more engaging conversations, and stronger community connections. The combination of gamification, personality matching, and real-world meetups fosters genuine friendships in a natural and fun way.
